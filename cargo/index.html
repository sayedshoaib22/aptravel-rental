About Our Cargo Division

Trusted Cargo & Logistics Partner in Mumbai

AP Travel & Rental extends its trusted transportation expertise into cargo and logistics — delivering fast, secure, and affordable goods transport solutions for businesses and individuals across India. Based in Sion, Mumbai, our cargo division leverages an established fleet, experienced drivers, and a nationwide network to move your shipments with care.

Whether you need a single parcel delivered across Maharashtra, commercial freight for your retail chain, or full truck load capacity for industrial materials, our team handles every shipment with the same professionalism that has made us a preferred bus rental provider in Mumbai for over a decade.

Why Businesses Choose AP Travel for Logistics

From Mumbai's bustling commercial hubs to remote delivery destinations, we combine route expertise with transparent pricing. Our cargo service covers domestic routes across Maharashtra, Karnataka, Goa, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and pan-India destinations — with real-time coordination and dedicated account support for recurring shipments.
